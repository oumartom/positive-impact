Our Clients and Partners

We have collaborated with diverse companies and organizations across various sectors.

BETNA

BETNA is a company specialized in managing high-end residences and villas. They requested our support for comprehensive administrative and financial management. Our interventions helped structure internal processes, improve financial flow management, and assist in formalizing contractual relations with partners and tenants.

Peschaud

Peschaud International is a logistics company with over 60 years of experience in complex geographic environments, operating in land, river, lake, and maritime transport. We assisted their teams in stock operational management and physical inventory. This mission improved stock data reliability, optimized equipment availability levels, and strengthened the monitoring of goods entries and exits in line with internal quality and safety standards.

Apex Audit Consulting

Apex Audit & Consulting, based in N'Djamena, offers accounting, auditing, and consulting services. Within our partnership, we provided expertise in reviewing and updating their financial management tools, strengthening the accounting team’s capacities, and improving internal procedures for optimal compliance with local fiscal and regulatory requirements.

Saphir

Saphir is a dynamic company involved in operational and administrative project management. Our mission included modernizing their accounting system and implementing digital tools to enhance daily management. We also trained their teams on new management practices to improve internal organizational efficiency.

ANAR-CSU

ANAR-CSU – Agency for the Regulation of Universal Health Coverage
We supported ANAR-CSU in a comprehensive accounting regularization and periodic assistance mission. Our interventions included:

  • Detailed review of accounting and administrative operations;
  • Regularization of opening entries and exhaustive transaction documentation;
  • Development and implementation of adapted management tools;
  • Payroll management assistance and tax/social declarations support;
  • Training and support of teams in mastering developed tools.
  • This work strengthened transparency, regulatory compliance, and the efficiency of the Agency's financial processes.

FIFTY Business

FIFTY Business is an innovative Chadian company founded in 2019 in N'Djamena. We had the pleasure of collaborating with them to provide tailored and modern solutions to their clients' challenges.

Our support focused on implementing innovative digital strategies aligned with international standards, contributing to the digitalization and enhanced performance of their operations.

This collaboration reflects our shared commitment to creating value through innovative digital solutions that meet the specific needs of the local and regional market.
