About Positive Impact

We turn ambitions into lasting results through innovative approaches and strategic collaborations.

Our Story

Founded in 2023 with the ambition to deliver innovative and sustainable business solutions, Positive Impact is a consulting firm based in Chad. Our committed team of professionals brings diverse expertise and a strong sense of quality to support every project with rigor and passion.

We understand that today’s business world demands both agility and responsibility. That’s why innovation and positive impact are at the heart of our approach. We help our clients tackle complex challenges while creating long-lasting economic and social value.

Our Mission

Empowering organizations by providing strategies, technologies, and innovative partnerships that generate lasting positive impact.

We are committed to designing solutions that address our clients’ current needs while supporting long-term transformations in their environment and sector.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a trusted partner in creating responsible solutions that deliver sustainable value to businesses and communities.

We aspire to a future where economic success naturally goes hand in hand with social and environmental commitment, benefiting all stakeholders.

Our Core Values

These core values guide everything we do and reflect who we are as an organization.

Innovation

We encourage creativity and bold thinking to design unique solutions for our clients’ challenges. Innovation drives our ability to create distinctive and lasting value.

Excellence

We strive to meet the highest standards of quality in our work and relationships. Excellence guides our commitments and ensures our partners’ satisfaction.

Sustainability

We place sustainability at the center of our actions. Our solutions combine immediate efficiency with long-term positive impact for businesses, communities, and the environment.

Partnership

We cultivate collaborations based on trust, listening, and mutual respect. Together with our clients, we build strong relationships to achieve ambitious results.

Openness

We embrace an open-minded and curious perspective, drawing on insights and best practices from diverse backgrounds to effectively address local and regional challenges.

Integrity

We always act with honesty and transparency. Ethical rigor is at the heart of our approach and guides our decisions every day.
