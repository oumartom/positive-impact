Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical process for engaging with Positive Impact?

Our engagement process typically begins with an initial consultation to understand your needs and objectives. We then develop a tailored proposal outlining our approach, timeline, and investment. Once approved, we move into the discovery phase, followed by strategy development, implementation, and ongoing evaluation and refinement.

How quickly can you start working on our project?

Our ability to start depends on the scope of your project and our current commitments. For most engagements, we can begin the initial consultation within 1-2 weeks of your inquiry. For urgent needs, we do our best to accommodate expedited timelines. We'll provide a specific timeline during our initial discussions.

Do you work with clients internationally?

Yes, we work with clients globally. With offices in North America, Europe, and Asia Pacific, and a team that speaks multiple languages, we're well-positioned to serve clients around the world. We use a combination of in-person and virtual collaboration tools to ensure effective communication regardless of location.

What industries do you specialize in?

We have experience across a wide range of industries, including financial services, retail & e-commerce, healthcare, manufacturing, education, technology, travel & hospitality, and non-profit. Our diverse team brings both industry-specific knowledge and cross-sector insights to every engagement.
